








DHS — Dhaka Health Service
“Modern healthcare, delivered to your doorstep”
 
Executive Summary
DHS — Dhaka Health Service is a modern, technology-driven home healthcare platform based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Modeled after UK systems like NHS, Cera Care, and Bupa, DHS combines online and offline services to deliver high-quality medical care, nursing, home assistance, and medicine delivery directly to patients’ homes. Starting in Uttara, DHS aims to expand citywide and eventually nationwide.
Mission & Vision
Mission: To make high-quality healthcare accessible, affordable, and reliable for every household in Dhaka.
Vision: To set the standard for home healthcare in Bangladesh by providing technology-integrated, patient-centric services.
Market Analysis
The home healthcare market in Dhaka is growing due to an aging population, increased chronic illnesses, and the demand for convenient care. By studying UK homecare models, DHS will integrate digital booking systems, verified staff, and quality assurance processes to meet these rising demands.
Core Services

Technology & Platform
DHS digital platform (website and mobile app) will include user login, booking management, prescription uploads, staff portal, admin dashboard, and analytics. Data encryption, SSL, and secure authentication will ensure patient privacy.
Staff Recruitment & Training
All doctors, nurses, and caregivers will undergo strict verification, police and NID checks, and mandatory training. Partnerships with medical colleges will ensure quality staff supply.
Medicine Delivery & Equipment Management
Partner pharmacies and equipment suppliers will integrate inventory with the DHS system. Delivery staff will be GPS-tracked for efficiency and timely service.
Revenue Model
Revenue streams include service commission, subscription plans, equipment rental, delivery fees, and corporate partnerships. Pricing will match market standards while maintaining high quality.
Marketing & Branding
Digital marketing (Google, Facebook), offline campaigns in hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies, uniformed staff, branded vehicles, and referral programs will establish trust and visibility.
Expansion Plan
Phase 1: Uttara, Phase 2: Major Dhaka areas, Phase 3: Entire Dhaka city, Phase 4: Nationwide expansion. Mobile app rollout and corporate partnerships in Phase 2+.
KPIs & Quality Assurance
Regular audits, patient feedback, staff performance monitoring, and service rating system will ensure UK-level quality standards.
Conclusion
DHS — Dhaka Health Service is designed to combine technology, professional care, and patient-centric service to set a new standard in Bangladesh’s home healthcare industry. By following UK-inspired models, rigorous recruitment, quality assurance, and strong digital integration, DHS will become the most trusted and efficient homecare provider in Dhaka and eventually nationwide.
